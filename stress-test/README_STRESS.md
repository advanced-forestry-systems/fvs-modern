Methodology and distilled results of the CONUS-scale FVS build/arm stress test
(source: /fs/scratch/PUOM0008/crsfaaron/fvs_stress, 756 GB, of which 743 GB are
regenerable per-plot projected tree-list CSVs that are NOT captured here).

drivers, plot-block manifests, the Bakuzis biological-realism pass/flag tables,
validation summaries, arm-comparison matrices, figures, and the Greg-arm stop
report. This preserves how the stress test was run and what it concluded without
the terabyte of intermediate tree lists.
